Communicating Scientific Credibility on Videnskab.dk


 Communication Design  & Master's Thesis, 2018


View prototype.

"Troværdighedssektionen" is an addition to Videnskab.dk which communicates the credibility of scientific articles, and is placed at the bottom of each health article on Videnskab.dk. The concept builds its credibility measurement on the foundation of three individual bibliometrics (BFI, hierarchies of evidence and h-index) and a journalistic tool, called ‘OBS!’. By utilizing these different measurements we give the user a broad overview of how trustworthy the results of a scientific health article is.
